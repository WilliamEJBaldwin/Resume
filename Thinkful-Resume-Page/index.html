About William

Working with other game designers gave me great pleasure while also teaching me good techniques for collaboration. After having 2 of my games published, I wanted to think about how I could showcase them better and build a website for my designs. I soon fell in love with the possibilities of web development and coding in general.

The feeling I had when 2 independent companies entrusted my work with so much of their capital, time, and effort was euphoric. I hope to gain that same respect and trust in my future employer as a developer.

Being a student at Thinkful, I am overjoyed with the path to my future as a software developer.

If you are looking for a developer with a thirst for knowledge that can fit your team, please reach out through my email. I am eager to join in, collaborate, problem solve, and learn. My favorite thing to eliminate in life: the possibility of disappointment!
